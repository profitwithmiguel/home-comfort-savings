12 Ways to Cut Your Power Bill by 20%

Updated 2025-09-03

  1. Switch to LED bulbs (save ~75% vs incandescents).
  2. Use a smart thermostat with eco schedules.
  3. Seal air leaks around doors and windows.
  4. Lower water heater setpoint to 120°F.
  5. Run laundry on cold and full loads.
  6. Add weatherstripping and door sweeps.
  7. Use ceiling fans to raise setpoint by 2°F.
  8. Replace clogged HVAC filters monthly.
  9. Unplug vampire loads or use smart plugs.
  10. Install a heat-pump water heater when replacing.
  11. Line-dry when possible.
  12. Claim rebates and tax credits for upgrades.
